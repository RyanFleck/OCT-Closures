About This Experiment

While taking a short course on XML, I decided to put together a small page to load and parse XML from an external source. That source is now OC-Transpo, as they provide a lot of data to the public. This page parses the Updates feed.

Update 1: The data on this page is NOT up to date. Just found out that XML cannot be loaded from another site client-side! This will now just be an experiment in processing and not a useful tool.

The work should be easy to replicate in NodeJS.

Update 2: XML now placed directly in index.
